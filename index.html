Celebrating 100 Years of Excellence in Timekeeping

Our Legacy

Anand Watches has stood as a symbol of sophistication, quality, and tradition in the world of watchmaking for over a century. Founded in 1924, our journey began with a simple vision: to craft timepieces that embody elegance and precision. Today, as a 100-year-old legacy, we continue to uphold the same commitment to excellence, blending modern innovation with classic craftsmanship.

The Origin Story

It all began in a small workshop where the first Anand watch was meticulously crafted by hand. With limited resources but unlimited ambition, Dhruvil and Harry set out to create watches that would stand the test of time. Their dedication to their craft quickly earned them a loyal following, and what started as a modest venture blossomed into one of the most respected names in watchmaking. The origin story of Anand Watches is one of resilience, passion, and a commitment to excellence that remains unwavering to this day.
